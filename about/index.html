一个能力跟不上理想的老男人

(An old man who can’t keep up with his ideal)

混迹社会很多年
觉得应该做自己想做的事情
那么我想做什么呢
我想让自己变得更好
于是乎
现在才开始健身
现在才开始学英语
现在才开始学编程
现在才关心自己和周围的人和事
也许有点晚
尽力而为吧

Live in society for many years
I feel I should do what I want to do.
So what do I want to do?
I want to make myself better
So
It’s only now that I’m getting fit.
It’s only now that we begin to learn English.
I’m just starting to learn programming now.
Now I care about myself and the people and things around me.
Maybe it’s a little late.
Do my best
